Intro

I have a passion for the crowd

I believe that the best way to make a better world is through crowd knowledge management. All my life I have been facinated with politics, media and the ideas that run our society. I have dedicate myself to understanding how information is spread, shared and developed by the crowd.

That search has lead me to some crazy places. I have been an intelligence operator with the Canadian Forces , director of Aborginal Affairs for the YLCM, martial artist, actor, model, activist and rails developer.

Huge fan of the MIT Center for Collective Intelligence and open academic projects like Code Academy, Edx, Khans Academy, Udacity... They have suplimented an build my education in innumerable ways. Right now the bulk of my attention is focused on delivering a cutting edge Crowd Knowledge Management Solution known as the OpenThink Lab project.

For my artistic focus right now I am currently focusing on Producing, Writing, Directing in acting in a Film Fest targeted ultra low buget online series based on Zen Master Dogen's Three Hundred Koans.
